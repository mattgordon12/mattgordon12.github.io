Privacy Policy for Pulse
Last Updated: February 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pulse ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you play our mobile game. By using Pulse, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use
To provide a functional and optimized gaming experience, we collect certain types of data:

Local Data (AsyncStorage): We use AsyncStorage to save your game progress, high scores, and settings locally on your device. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Device Identifiers: We may collect unique device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID or Apple IDFA) to facilitate personalized advertising through third-party services.

2. Third-Party Services: Google AdMob
Pulse uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to show ads that are more relevant to you. This data includes:

Device ID and IP address.

Approximate location data.

User interaction with ads.

For more information, please review the Google Privacy & Terms.

3. Data Protection Rights
GDPR (European Economic Area)
If you are a resident of the EEA, you have the following rights:

Right to Access/Delete: You can request access to or deletion of your data.

Consent: We only process personalized ads for EEA users after obtaining explicit consent via a consent management platform.

Withdrawal: You can withdraw consent at any time through your device settings.

CCPA (California)
If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

Know what personal information is being collected.

Request the deletion of your personal information.

Opt-Out: You have the right to opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. You can do this by limiting ad tracking in your iOS or Android system settings.

4. Children’s Privacy
Pulse does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). If we discover that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
